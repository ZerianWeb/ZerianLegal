Zerian Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Zerian ("we", "our", or "us") operates a Discord bot designed to enhance server functionality through moderation, automation, and utility features. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Zerian, we may collect and store the following information:

  • User Data: Discord User IDs
  • Server Data: Guild IDs, roles, and configuration settings
  • Channel Data: Channel IDs for logging and automation
  • Message Data: Limited message content only when required for commands or moderation
  • Usage Data: Commands used and interaction logs

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly to:

  • Provide and maintain bot functionality
  • Execute commands requested by users
  • Improve features and performance
  • Prevent abuse, spam, and misuse

4. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored securely using industry-standard practices. While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide services. Data may be deleted upon request or when it is no longer required.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Data may only be shared:

  • If required by law or legal obligation
  • To protect the security and integrity of the bot

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

8. Third-Party Services

Zerian relies on third-party services such as Discord’s API. These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies.

9. Children’s Privacy

Zerian does not knowingly collect data from individuals under the age required by Discord’s Terms of Service.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of Zerian indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Information

If you have any questions or requests regarding your data, please contact us through the official Zerian Discord support server.
